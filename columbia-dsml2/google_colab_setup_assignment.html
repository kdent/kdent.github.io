Prepare Your Development Environment

Create a folder on your Google drive where you will store data files to use during class. You may be asked to sign in to your Google account if you are not signed in already.

  1. Go to https://drive.google.com/drive/my-drive From there create a new folder called "columbia-ml-data", and make it your current directory.
  2. Download the file iris.csv to your machine and then upload it to the columbia-ml-data directory you just created on your Google drive.
  3. Next, go to https://colab.research.google.com/ and create a new notebook. Then write the code from the slide in the classroom to confirm that your environment is working correctly.
