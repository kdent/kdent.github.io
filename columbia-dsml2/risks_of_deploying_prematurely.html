The Risks of Deploying Technology Prematurely

For this assignment you will read about the media company CNET's use of generative AI to create news stories. As you read think about choices they made and things they might or might not have done differently.

Read the following story from The Verge, CNET found errors in more than half of its AI-written stories.

When you have finished that story, read the following story from The Conversation, Newsrooms are experimenting with generative AI, warts and all.
