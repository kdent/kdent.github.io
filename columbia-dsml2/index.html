Decision Trees (notebook + decision tree assignment (in slides and canvas) 40 minutes)
For the assignment:

Group Activity

  • Work in pairs or small groups.
  • Draw your tree on a piece of paper or a whiteboard.
  • Each leaf of the tree should predict one fruit type.
  • Compare your decision tree with those made by other groups.

Discussion Questions

  • What feature did you split on first? Why?
  • Did other groups build different trees?
  • Was your tree the shortest or simplest possible?
